THE PANDEMIC

The COVID-19 pandemic, caused by the novel coronavirus SARS-CoV-2, has been a transformative global event that has gripped the world since its emergence in late 2019. This highly contagious and initially mysterious virus has fundamentally altered our way of life, healthcare systems, and economies. COVID-19 has exposed the vulnerabilities of our interconnected world, underlining the importance of public health measures, scientific research, and international cooperation. From unprecedented lockdowns to the rapid development of vaccines, it has forced societies to adapt and innovate at an extraordinary pace. The pandemic has raised critical questions about preparedness for future global health crises and the importance of collective action to mitigate the impact of such events.

Through the following visualizations, the aim is to deliver useful information regarding the Recovered, Active cases as well as Deaths back in 2020 with special focus on the situation in Australia.

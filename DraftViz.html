THE PANDEMIC

COVID-19, caused by the novel coronavirus SARS-CoV-2, has profoundly impacted the global landscape since its emergence in late 2019. This visualization provides a concise overview of the pandemic's key metrics and trends. Through compelling data representations, it aims to illustrate the virus's spread, infection rates, vaccination progress, and the societal changes it has prompted. By presenting this information visually, we can better understand the virus's trajectory, the effectiveness of public health measures, and the efforts made to combat it. This visualization offers a snapshot of our world's ongoing battle against COVID-19, encapsulating the challenges and resilience displayed by individuals and communities worldwide.

The Map

This visualization aims to show the COVID-19 stats in different countries and how many lives were affected because of this deadly virus. After moving the mouse over the map, all the different countries are highlighted and colored according to per 10,000 confirmed cases.

The Recovered(%) vs Total Deaths(%)

This visualization aims to show all the different countries and how they differ in the number of people recovered vs dead from the pandemic. Pinch in or out to have a closer look at the scatter plot and change the x-axis accordingly.

Affecting Major Countries

This visualization was mainly aimed to show the drastic effects of the Pandemic on Major Countries of the World.

Total Cases vs Total Deaths

In this visualization, different continents are taken into consideration to compare the Total Cases noted which weren't able to recover. The Bandwidth and the resolve can be changed to zoom in and view the chart in a more understandable manner.
